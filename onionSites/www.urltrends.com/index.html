All New Trend Reports

Our recently redesigned Trend Report will allow you to view vital search engine information about any url that you enter. The Trend Report will allow you to see the Urls link popularity, PageRank™, Alexa Rank, keywords that you can find the site by and more.

The redesign of the Trend Reports have made them much easier to digest. The two largest changes are the more informative charts and the long single page has been broken up onto multiple pages. Oh, and did we mention more information has been added? We now track over 50 different pieces of information about every url in our systems. And yes, the Trend Reports still do what they have always done, they will track the trends of the url over time and display them to you.

An unlimited number of trend reports can be generated without registering and at no cost, however if you register you can track these reports via the My UrlTrends interface, email or even through a personalized RSS feed. If you would like the information updated on a weekly basis we offer paid plans that will allow you to track an unlimited number of urls.

To generate a Trend Report enter a url into the text field above, or if you just want to find urls that are related to a certain subject, enter a search term.

  • SEO Report

    Our SEO Report includes not only the hard data, but also a complete, easy to read, plain English analysis that explains exactly what the data means and how it affects the site's search engine rankings. It is suitable for both Webmasters and SEO companies alike. If you are a professional SEO, you'll be pleased to know that we even offer versions branded with your companies information and all references to UrlTrends removed. The SEO report is similar to our Trend Reports, however it goes into much deeper depth about each of the rankings and provides you with even more charts and data.

  • Keyword Report

    Want to know every keyword that your competitor ranks for in Google, Yahoo! and MSN? Yeah, we thought so. Our brand new Keyword Report tells you all of this and more. Note: you must know who your competitor is to purchase this report. If you do not know all of your competitors, try our Competition Report below.

  • Competition Report

    How would you like to know every competitor that a site has? Even better, how would you like to know the keywords of each of those sites? Well, our brand spankin' new Competition Report does just that! This report will tell you each of the sites that were found to be competing for rankings in Google, Yahoo! and MSN, then it'll tell you each of the keywords and positions that each competitor ranks for! The competitive edge that you'll get will be priceless! You have the choice of two formats, Competition Report Lite and Competition Report Full.

  • Trend Comparison Tool

    Compare almost any of the data we collect for up to 100 URLs at once. It's the ultimate way to see the exact search engine information that you want to see in one page. Allows you to export the results to Excel (CSV) or PDF.

  • Reverse Keyword Lookup Tool

    This tool has been designed to allow you to find out what popular terms a URL is ranking in Google, Yahoo! and MSN for. To use this tool, enter a list of URLs and then our Reverse Keyword Lookup tool will display all of keywords that you can find the URLs by. To our knowledge, this is the only tool online that allows you to see all of your competitors keywords, their positions for those keywords and the overall competition for those keywords in Google's, Yahoo's and MSN's organic search engine results. Allows you to export the results to Excel (CSV) or XML.

  • PageRank Commonality

    This tool was originally created for one of our Partners, and by popular demand is now available to you. This tool is rather simple, you give it a URL and it'll go and check all of Google's Data Centers and tell you what that DC reports your PR as. Then it will look at all of the results and tell you the Common PageRank. You know that if you are watching PR, it fluctuates and gives you incorrect results sometimes, this tool will eliminate false positives. As an added bonus, it will tell you the Alexa Rank for the URL too!

  • HTTP Header Lookup Tool

    This is one of the simplest tools we offer. You enter a URL and we will instantly check the URL and then display to you the HTTP Headers that are sent back to us.

  • PageRank™ buttons

    Put an image on your website and automatically display your current PageRank.
